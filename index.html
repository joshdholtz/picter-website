All you need is an idea

You don't need to be a designer or developer to create an app with Picter. We have a lot creativity and experience here at Picter and we can take your idea and turn it into a working product.

Quick turnaround

All we have to do is take your designs and your selected overlay images and put them with the main pieces of the app that we have pre-built. We then get your approval and ship the app off to the app stores. Note: it will take more time for apps that need more customization

Give your users something fun

Your fans, customers, and attendees will have a great app to share their experiences at your venue with their social circle. The pictures they produce with your app will have be branded for your organization.

What Our Apps Look Like

Overlay Selection

This is where you can let your users select from any overlay images of your choosing. The most popular choices are event logos, team logos, or team mascots. But be creative, its your app.

Sharing Options

Your users have options to give the picture a caption, save the image to their device, and also share their experience to a handfull of social networks.

Public Interaction

Each picture has a public page that anyone can view. This page gets all the attention and allows for fan, attendee, or customer interaction.
